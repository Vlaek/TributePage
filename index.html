Kanye West

The man who saved a billion lives

Here's a timeline of Kanye West's life:

  • 1977 - Born in Atlanta, Georgia.
  • 1990 - Whet the rapper was 13 years old, his mother paid for the recording of his first song in a basement studio.
  • 1994 - Kanye graduated from Polaris High School in 1994. The rapper received a scholarship to attend Chicago's American Academy of Art. He studied painting while at the university. He then transferred to Chicago State University.
  • 1996 - Kanye West appeared at the Fat Beats storefront in Greenwich Village. While he was at the storefront, Kanye dropped a freestyle that became very famous.
  • 1998 - Kanye West gets a taste of the industry in 1998 when he sells his first beat to local Chicago rapper Gravity. The rapper bought the beat from Kanye Wes for $8,000. Kanye West then sold a beat to Jermain Dupri for $5,000. Shortly after these achievements, Kanye West dropped out of Chicago State University to do music on a full-time basis.
  • 2000 - Kanye West's career shift into high gear in 2000. This year marked the start of the decade that Rolling Stone believes made Kanye West the icon that he now is.
  • 2001 - West got cemented into his career in 2001 because of his contributions to Jay-Z's 'The Blueprint'. The rapper produced five tracks for the album.
  • 2004 - Kanye West's debut album, 'The College Dropout' was a massive success. The album sold 441,000 units in its first week. The album gets certified 3x platinum.
  • 2005 - Kanye West went on to win two Grammy Awards for The College Dropout.
  • 2007 - Kanye West scheduled September 11, 2007, as the release date of his third studio album, 'Graduation'.
  • 2008 - The rapper released '808s & Heartbreak' in November 2008. The dark sound of this album is due to the struggles the rapper was having with the death of his mother and the end of a relationship that West was grieving.
  • 2009 - Kanye West partnered with Nike in 2009 to release the Air Yeezys.
  • 2010 - Rapper released 'My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy'.
  • 2012 - Kanye realesed 'Cruel Summer'.
  • 2013 - West released his most polarizing album, Yeezus. While the album was extremely unorthodox, it still debuted at number one.
  • 2014 - Kanye West got married to Kim Kardashian in 104 at a private ceremony in Italy.
  • 2015 - debuted his Adidas line Yeezy Season 1. The shoe line would go on to become extremely popular throughout the world.
  • 2019 - Kanye West revealed that he had met God. The rapper starred to have his popular Sunday Services privately and then started to have the event across the world. The rapper releases his first album since his conversion, 'Jesus is King'. The album features Christian raps and popular rap artists.
  • 2020 - Kanye West announced that he would be running for the office of the President. At one of his first campaign rally, Kanye West seemingly has a breakdown and shares very personal information that he later had to apologize to his wife for making.

"I'm not loving you, the way I wanted to What I had to do, had to run from you I'm in love with you but the vibe is wrong And that haunted me, all the way home So you never know, never never know, Never know enough, 'til it's over love."
